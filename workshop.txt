title=Swap It
description= [b]Does the following:[/b]
description= [list]
description= [*]Equipped weapons will swap places with hotslotted weapons instead of being put away entirely.
description= [*] Example: If you have Weapon A on your belt and Weapon B in hand- selecting weapon A will equip weapon A and put weapon B on your belt.
description= [*]Activating an empty hotslot will attach the equipped item if possible.
description= [*]Individual hotslots can be toggled via options menu.
description= [/list]
description=
description=
description= [b]Supports:[/b] G.E.A.R.
description=
description= [b]Warning:[/b] Other mods that interact with hotslots or item equipping may cause issues- please post a comment describing the issue and any mods you may also use. Patches for said mods may be possible.
