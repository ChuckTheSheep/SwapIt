title=Swap It
description= Does the following:
description= - Equipped weapons will swap places with hotslotted weapons instead of being put away entirely.
description= -- Example: If you have Weapon A on your belt and Weapon B in hand- selecting weapon A will equip weapon A and put weapon B on your belt.
description= - Activating an empty hotslot will attach the equipped item if possible.
description= 
description= SwapIt - applies SwapIt to all hotslots.
description= SwapItGEARPatch - applies SwapIt to all hotslots but also maintain's GEAR's hotslot mechanics.
description= SwapIt1Only -  applies SwapIt only to hotslot 1.
description= 
description= Multiple options on at once does not work.
description=
description= Warning: Other mods that interact with hotslots or item equipping may cause issues- please post a comment describing the issue and any mods you may also use. Patches for said mods may be possible.
description=
